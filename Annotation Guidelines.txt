Complication: An event, or series of events, that points at the problems, or tensions, in the story. A hypothetical or plausible complication is also labelled as such.

Resolution: Refers to the way the story is resolved or to the release of tension. An improvement from, or a manner of coping with, an existing or hypothetical state/situation will also be considered to be a Resolution (even if temporary or partial). Resolution also includes an intention, assessment, anticipation, potential, or desire for resolution.

Success: indication of an achievement or a desirable outcome. A probable, potential, conditional or anticipated success - is also tagged as Success. An entity described by the text in a successful way will be tagged as Success.
